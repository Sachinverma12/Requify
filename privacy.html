Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us.

Last updated: January 2024

1. Information We Collect

When you use Requify, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details you provide during registration.
  • Property Information: Details about properties you list, search for, or inquire about.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform, including search queries and browsing patterns.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our real estate services
  • To connect buyers, sellers, and renters
  • To send you property recommendations and updates
  • To improve our platform and user experience
  • To communicate with you about your account and inquiries
  • To ensure security and prevent fraud

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Property Owners: When you express interest in a property, your contact details may be shared with the owner.
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist us in operating our platform.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure authentication with JWT tokens
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Export your data in a portable format

6. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your session and authentication status
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze platform usage and performance

7. Children's Privacy

Requify is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page.
